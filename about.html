Our Story

From Pollachi with Love ❤️

Mugavari Thazhai isn't just a brand; it's a bridge between the lush coconut groves of Pollachi and your home.

Our Roots

Born in the heart of Pollachi, famously known as the "Coconut Capital of Tamil Nadu," our journey began with a simple mission: to bring back the authentic taste of traditional oils.

In a world of refined and processed goods, we stood for something real. We partnered with local farmers to source the finest coconuts and groundnuts, ensuring that every drop of oil is pressed using the traditional 'Chekku' (wood-pressed) method.

Our Mission

To deliver purity, health, and sustainability. We believe that what you eat defines who you are, and we want to ensure you have access to chemical-free, nutrient-rich oils.

Beyond oils, we are committed to sustainability. Our eco-friendly coconut shell crafts are proof that nature's gifts are endless if we use them wisely.

About the Founder

Founder – Mugavari Thazhai

Mugavari Thazhai was founded with a simple yet powerful vision — to reconnect people with pure, traditional, and trustworthy products from the heart of Pollachi.

Growing up close to nature and local farming practices, the founder understood the value of traditional cold-pressed oils, natural food processing, ethical sourcing and fair trade.

What started as a personal belief soon became a mission — to make authentic, chemical-free products accessible to everyone, without losing their traditional essence.

“Quality is not a feature. It is a responsibility.”

The founder continues to guide Mugavari Thazhai with a strong focus on purity, transparency, and sustainability.
